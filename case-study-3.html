A platform redesign focused on unifying menu management workflows across three distinct touchpoints, reducing operational overhead and enabling real-time synchronization.

01

The Challenge

Restaurant operators managed menus separately across the app, POS, and web portal — leading to inconsistencies, manual errors, and hours of duplicated effort each week.

02

Research & Discovery

Conducted interviews with 12 restaurant managers to map pain points. Shadowed operators during peak hours to understand the real workflow. Audited 3 existing interfaces side-by-side to identify redundancies.

03

Design Approach

Defined a single source-of-truth model where changes propagate in real time. Prototyped 3 structural variations, tested with 6 operators, and iterated toward a master-menu paradigm with platform-specific overrides.

04

Solution

Designed a unified menu management hub with a master catalogue, real-time sync indicators, and per-platform override controls. Introduced bulk editing, conflict resolution prompts, and a change log for accountability.

05

Results

Launched to 200+ restaurant partners across 3 platforms. Menu update time dropped from ~45 minutes to under 5 minutes per change cycle.

improvement in menu management efficiency across all platforms
